OUR STORY

Yummy in my Tummy first started in 2014 as a business from home, baking up all kind of cakes, cupcakes, cookies, pies and Themed cakes.

I started as a family bakeshop. Local customers fell in love. Word got out. Fast forward two generations and our artistry and quality continue to set the trend. Our experience is admired though not easily replicate.

Over the years our business has grown to include a talented team of professional bakers, decorators and customer service ambassadors. Even still, at the heart of it all we are a family bakery dedicated to bringing you sweet memories.

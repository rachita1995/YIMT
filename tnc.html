Terms And Conditions

Deposits and cancellation: Your initial deposit reserves your event date and order and is non-refundable and non-transferable. For orders over Rs500 the remainder of your balance must be paid 1 month before your event. The balance on smaller orders can be settled on collection/delivery. If we do not receive the balance as expected the contract can be considered null and void, and deposit will not be refunded. The total cost is non-refundable if your order is cancelled within 1 month of the order due date. If The Little Cake Parlour has to cancel your order due to ill health/death/ then you will be refunded in full and every attempt will be made to find an alternate baker for your cake.

Your cake is baked to be at optimum freshness on the date of your event (if no event date has been specified then the date of collection or delivery is assumed to be the event date). Your cake will last for a few days after collection, but can be frozen for up to 3 months. The Little Cake Parlour cannot guarantee the quality or freshness of cakes after the ‘best before’ date.

The date of your final payment is the final date for any changes of size, style or flavours of the cake. Any changes requested after this date cannot be guaranteed. Changes may be subject to additional charges.

Delivery and setup is made according to any directions given by the client; i.e. to the address (venue) listed, on the date indicated by email.

The Little Cake Parlour can take absolutely no responsibility for any damage to your cake after delivery and setup are complete or after you collect from our studio. If you are having your cake delivered to a venue when you are not there we will photograph the cake once it is arranged at your venue as evidence of our completed work. You/your venue (on your behalf) are responsible for providing an appropriate and secure table and environment for the cake(s). Tiered cakes are heavy and require a sturdy table, and optimal room temperature of 15C-23C. The Little Cake Parlour will not be held accountable for any damage incurred to your cake by event staff/family members/pets.
